# Product Launch Roadmap - Example

**Product:** MAXIMUS Personal Assistant
**Launch Date:** TBD
**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## Phase 1: Preparation (2 days)

### Step 1: Create Landing Page
- **Duration:** 4 hours
- **Assignee:** Marketing Team
- **Tools:** Webflow, Figma
- **Tasks:**
  - Design hero section
  - Add feature highlights
  - Create demo video section
  - Add pricing page
  - Setup contact form

### Step 2: Prepare Visual Assets
- **Duration:** 3 hours
- **Assignee:** Design Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Product screenshots
  - Demo GIFs
  - Social media banners
  - Thumbnail for videos

### Step 3: Write Copy and Descriptions
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Assignee:** Content Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Product Hunt description
  - HackerNews post
  - Reddit post
  - Twitter thread
  - LinkedIn post

### Step 4: Setup Analytics
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Assignee:** Tech Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Google Analytics
  - Mixpanel events
  - Hotjar heatmaps

---

## Phase 2: Soft Launch (1 day)

### Step 5: Beta Release
- **Duration:** 4 hours
- **Assignee:** Tech Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Deploy to production
  - Invite 50 beta users
  - Setup feedback form
  - Monitor errors

### Step 6: Collect Initial Feedback
- **Duration:** 4 hours
- **Assignee:** Product Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Review user feedback
  - Fix critical bugs
  - Improve onboarding
  - Update documentation

---

## Phase 3: Public Launch (1 day)

### Step 7: Product Hunt Launch
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Assignee:** Marketing Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Submit to Product Hunt (8am)
  - Respond to all comments
  - Thank supporters
  - Share on social media

### Step 8: HackerNews Post
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Assignee:** Tech Lead
- **Tasks:**
  - Write "Show HN" post
  - Answer technical questions
  - Engage with community

### Step 9: Reddit Posts
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Assignee:** Community Manager
- **Subreddits:**
  - r/SideProject
  - r/ProductManagement
  - r/MachineLearning
  - r/Python

### Step 10: Social Media Blast
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Assignee:** Social Media Manager
- **Platforms:**
  - Twitter thread
  - LinkedIn post
  - Facebook groups
  - Discord communities

---

## Phase 4: Post-Launch (Ongoing)

### Step 11: Respond to Comments
- **Duration:** 2 hours/day for 1 week
- **Assignee:** All hands
- **Channels:**
  - Product Hunt
  - HackerNews
  - Reddit
  - Twitter
  - Email

### Step 12: Collect and Analyze Feedback
- **Duration:** Ongoing
- **Assignee:** Product Team
- **Tasks:**
  - Review all feedback
  - Prioritize features
  - Create roadmap
  - Plan next iteration

### Step 13: Monitor Metrics
- **Duration:** Daily
- **Assignee:** Growth Team
- **Metrics:**
  - Signups
  - Active users
  - Retention rate
  - Feature usage
  - Feedback scores
